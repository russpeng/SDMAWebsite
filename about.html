About SDMBA

Improving trail access since 1994.

Improve Trail Access. Encourage Stewardship. Build Community. That is our mission, and it has guided everything we do for three decades.

The San Diego Mountain Biking Association is a volunteer-driven nonprofit and one of the largest regional trail advocacy organizations in the United States. With over 1,700 members, we work to protect trail access, build sustainable trails, and bring riders together across San Diego County.

Land Acknowledgment

The San Diego Mountain Biking Association acknowledges that we ride, work, and gather on the unceded territory of the Kumeyaay, Payomkawichum, Kuupiaxchem, and Cahuilla peoples. We honor the 18 federally recognized tribal nations with reservations in San Diego County and commit to respectful stewardship of these lands.
