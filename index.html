We built our own road.

Our trails are living proof.

From Black Mountain to Noble Canyon, SDMBA volunteers carve, maintain, and protect the singletrack that riders dream about — one shovel full at a time.

Over 1,500 members have joined the San Diego Mountain Biking Association to provide a unified voice for mountain biking. We build positive relationships with land managers, support sustainable trails, and educate riders to respect the environment and recreate responsibly.

What We Do

Building better trails.
Stronger community.

01

Trail Advocacy

Working with land managers and decision-makers to protect and expand public trail access across the county.

02

Trail Stewardship

Hands-on trail building and maintenance that keeps systems sustainable and rideable for everyone.

03

Community Building

Group rides, meetups, clinics, and events bringing riders together from every corner of San Diego.

04

Education

Teaching responsible trail etiquette and environmental awareness to preserve trails for tomorrow.

05

E-MTB Leadership

Guiding the conversation on electric mountain bike access with thoughtful, fact-based advocacy.

06

Conservation

Balancing recreation with conservation—supporting sustainable opportunities that respect open space.
